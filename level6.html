BSc (Hons) Games Technologies (Top-Up) (Full-Time)

Course Overview

This top-up degree is designed for those with a relevant foundation degree or equivalent qualification. You'll advance your knowledge in game development with a focus on emerging technologies and industry practices.

Qualifications

  • BSc (Hons) Games Technologies
  • Bachelor's Degree (Level 6)
  • University Centre programme

Assessment Methods

  • Project-based assessments
  • Technical portfolios
  • Industry placements
  • Final capstone project

Course Details

Length: 1 year (Full-Time)
Start Date: 7th September 2026
Location: Rye Hill Campus
Study Mode: University Centre

Trips and Activities

International industry partnerships, advanced workshops, networking with studios, and opportunities for game showcase presentations.
