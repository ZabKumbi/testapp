Games Development - Level 3

Course Overview

A comprehensive college-level programme for ages 16-18. Build on foundational knowledge with intermediate skills in game design, programming, and production. This two-year course emphasizes hands-on experience and industry-relevant projects.

Qualifications

  • Extended Diploma in Games Development (Level 3)
  • Equivalent to A-levels

Assessment Methods

  • Group projects
  • Individual portfolios
  • Presentations

Course Details

Level: 3
Length: 2 years
Study Mode: Full-Time
Start Date: 1st September 2026
Location: Rye Hill Campus
Age Range: 16-18 College

Trips and Activities

Attend conferences, work on real client projects, and participate in national competitions to build your professional network.
