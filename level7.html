MSc Games Design and Development

Course Overview

Our postgraduate MSc programme prepares you for leadership roles in the games industry. Combining advanced technical knowledge with management skills, this degree focuses on innovation and cutting-edge game development practices.

Qualifications

  • MSc Games Design and Development
  • Master's Degree (Level 7)
  • University Centre programme

Assessment Methods

  • Advanced project development
  • Research dissertations
  • Industry consultancy projects
  • Professional presentations

Course Details

Length: 1 year (Full-Time)
Start Date: 7th September 2026
Location: Rye Hill Campus
Study Mode: University Centre (International)

Trips and Activities

Industry partnerships, global conferences, advanced research opportunities, mentoring from industry leaders, and opportunities to present at international game development forums.
