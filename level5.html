FdSc Games Design and Development

Course Overview

This Foundation Degree offers comprehensive training in games design and development with a university-centre focus. Designed for international and local students, it combines practical skills with theoretical knowledge for a career in the games industry.

Qualifications

  • FdSc Games Design and Development
  • Foundation Degree (Level 5)
  • University Centre programme

Assessment Methods

  • Project-based assignments
  • Portfolio development
  • Practical demonstrations
  • Industry collaboration projects

Course Details

Length: 2 years (Full-Time)
Start Date: 7th September 2026
Location: Rye Hill Campus
Study Mode: University Centre (International)

Trips and Activities

Industry studio visits, game development conferences, international networking events, and guest lectures from leading professionals in the games industry.
